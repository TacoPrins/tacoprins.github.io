Hello! I am Taco Prins, a final-year PhD candidate in Environmental Economics at the MInt section of the University of Amsterdam, under the supervision of [Rick van der Ploeg](https://rickvanderploeg.wordpress.com/) and [Ton van den Bremer](https://sites.google.com/view/tonvandenbremer/home).

## Research
My research focuses on climate change as a macroeconomic risk and optimal climate adaptation. The first research project of my PhD has been a paper with my supervisors on optimal flood protection. We provide quantitative results calibrated to the Netherlands. A second paper in progress, with fellow PhD student [Sevim Dinlemez](https://www.uva.nl/en/profile/d/i/s.e.g.dinlemez/s.e.g.dinlemez.html), studies heterogeneous beliefs over climate risk in the housing market.  

## Curriculum Vitae
Prior to my PhD, I completed a BA in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics and an MPhil in Economics, both at the University of Oxford. I also have work experience at several Dutch policy institutions.
